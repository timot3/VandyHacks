Our results do not look very similar to the categories due to mode collapse . In short, our model is outputting a limited range of results since our discriminator is not enforcing more diversity in the generator, and as a result, the generator is collapsing to a single point.



    This ended up being our "alarm clock" output.



    This is the latent space of our generator, showing the different points it is generating for the discriminator to train on. The lack of diversity in our generator is why our model failed to produce meaningful results, which is why all of the graphs look identical.
